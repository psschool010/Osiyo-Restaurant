Our Story

A celebration of Uzbek heritage

Osiyo Grand is more than a restaurant — it is a gathering place where generations of Uzbek culinary tradition come alive. Every dish is prepared with time-honored recipes, the finest local ingredients, and an unwavering commitment to authenticity.

From intimate family dinners to grand banquets, we craft every experience with the same devotion: to honour the flavours, hospitality, and culture of Uzbekistan.
